About Gellifique

Information provided by various external sources

GellifiQue LTD aims to provide premium quality nail art supplies to fully qualified nail technicians and those in training. We pride ourselves in delivering these products at affordable prices in order to create an equal opportunity for nail technicians of all backgrounds to have access to quality products thus helping them to achieve a higher customer retention rate and ultimately supporting them in their career.
